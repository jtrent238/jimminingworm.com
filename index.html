My Minecraft Mods

- ObsTrophies

ObsTrophies is a mod for Minecraft 1.7.10 that adds Trophy drops for mobs added by other Mods. Defeat a mob and there's a chance it will drop a trophy! There are also some unique swords available to increase the chance of you getting a trophy.

For more details see the Minecraft Forum thread here

Downloads are available on CurseForge here Don't forget to get ObsCore as well! It's required for ObsAoA to work!
